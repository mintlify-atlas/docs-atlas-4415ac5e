ChatGPT Exporter is a free, open-source Tampermonkey userscript that enhances the ChatGPT interface with powerful export capabilities. Once installed, a new **Export** button appears in the ChatGPT sidebar, letting you save any conversation — or all your conversations at once — in the format of your choice.

## Why ChatGPT Exporter?

ChatGPT doesn't provide a built-in way to export individual conversations in readable formats. ChatGPT Exporter fills that gap — it injects seamlessly into the ChatGPT UI and gives you full control over your conversation history.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Tampermonkey">
    Install the Tampermonkey browser extension for [Chrome](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/tampermonkey/dhdgffkkebhmkfjojejmpbldmpobfkfo), [Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/tampermonkey), or [Edge](https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/detail/tampermonkey/iikmkjmpaadaobahmlepeloendndfphd).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the userscript">
    Click **Install** on [Greasy Fork](https://greasyfork.org/scripts/456055-chatgpt-exporter) or install directly from [GitHub](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pionxzh/chatgpt-exporter/master/dist/chatgpt.user.js).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open ChatGPT">
    Navigate to [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) and open any conversation. You'll see the **Export** button in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export your conversation">
    Click the **Export** button, choose a format (Markdown, HTML, PNG, JSON, or Text), and the file downloads immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  ChatGPT Exporter works with both ChatGPT Free and ChatGPT Plus accounts. It also supports ChatGPT shared/public conversation pages.
</Note>
