ChatGPT Exporter is a free, open-source Tampermonkey userscript that solves a straightforward problem: ChatGPT has no built-in way to save your conversations in a usable format. Once installed, the script injects an **Export** button directly into the ChatGPT sidebar. From there you can save any conversation — or hundreds of them at once — in the format that suits your workflow.

## How it works

ChatGPT Exporter runs as a Tampermonkey userscript — a small JavaScript program that your browser loads alongside any page you visit. When you navigate to `chatgpt.com`, Tampermonkey injects the script into the page, which adds the **Export** button to the sidebar navigation. The script reads conversation data directly from the page and converts it into your chosen format without sending anything to external servers.

<Note>
  ChatGPT Exporter requires the Tampermonkey browser extension. It works with ChatGPT Free, ChatGPT Plus, and shared/public conversation pages.
</Note>
