Once ChatGPT Exporter is installed, exporting a conversation takes seconds. This guide walks you through your first export — from opening a conversation to downloading the file. If you haven't installed the script yet, start with the [Installation guide](/installation).

## Export a single conversation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a conversation in ChatGPT">
    Navigate to [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) and open any conversation from the sidebar. The conversation must have at least one message for the export to work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the Export button">
    In the left sidebar, look for the **Export** item (with an arrow-out icon) near the bottom of the navigation. Click it to open the export menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your format">
    The export menu shows all available options:

    - **Copy Text** — copies the conversation as plain text to your clipboard
    - **Screenshot** — saves a PNG image of the conversation
    - **Markdown** — downloads a `.md` file
    - **HTML** — downloads a self-contained `.html` file
    - **JSON** — opens a sub-menu with OpenAI format, TavernAI JSONL, and Ooba options
    - **Export All** — opens the bulk export dialog

    Click the format you want.
  </Step>
  <Step title="File downloads automatically">
    For file-based formats (Markdown, HTML, JSON, PNG), the file downloads to your browser's default downloads folder immediately. For **Copy Text**, the conversation is copied to your clipboard — no file is created.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The default filename uses your conversation title and export date. You can customize the filename pattern in **Settings** — see [Filename Format](/features/filename-format) for details.
</Tip>

## Export as a screenshot (PNG)

The **Screenshot** option captures the full conversation as rendered by your browser.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the conversation you want to capture">
    Make sure the conversation is fully loaded in the browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Screenshot in the export menu">
    The script temporarily adjusts styles to ensure clean rendering, then uses `html2canvas` to capture the conversation thread.
  </Step>
  <Step title="PNG downloads to your downloads folder">
    The PNG is saved with your configured filename format. The image reflects the current dark or light mode of your ChatGPT interface.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The PNG export captures the conversation as rendered in the browser DOM. Very long conversations are captured in full — not just the visible portion.
</Note>
